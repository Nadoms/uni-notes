# Guidelines
## A. Cheating
All runs must follow the [rules of the speedrun.com Minecraft Any% Glitchless leaderboard](https://rules.minecraftspeedrunning.com/latest).
1. Do not abuse Minecraft bugs disallowed under Section D.1.2 of the [speedrun.com rules](https://rules.minecraftspeedrunning.com/latest).
    - This will result in a ban from accessing MCSR Ranked, ranging from 7 days to permanent, depending on the severity of the offense.
2. Do not abuse bugs within the MCSR Ranked mod.
    - This will result in a ban from accessing MCSR Ranked, ranging from 7 days to permanent, depending on the severity of the offense.
3. Do not attempt to inject cheats into MCSR Ranked.
    - This will result in a permanent ban from accessing MCSR Ranked.

## B. Matchmaking Abuse
MCSR Ranked works on Elo to provide balanced matchmaking to its players. The players are expected to uphold this, and not manipulate their Elo to be something that it isn't.

1. Do not lower your Elo outside of your skill range to play against easier opponents. (**Smurfing**)
    - This will result in a queue ban or a ban from accessing MCSR Ranked, ranging from 7 days to 30 days.
    - Smurfing is defined as consistently playing at an Elo range where you significantly outpace your opponents in almost every game, negatively impacting the ranked experience for said opponents.
2. Do not play on another player's account to increase their Elo. (**Boosting**)
    - For all involved, this will result in a permanent ban from accessing MCSR Ranked.
3. Do not play Ranked mode on an alternative account, unless granted explicit permission from the admins. (**Alting**)
    - For the "main" account, this will result in a ban from accessing MCSR Ranked, ranging from 30 days to permanent.
    - For any alternative accounts, this will result in a permanent ban from accessing MCSR Ranked.
4. Do not repeatedly go AFK during your matches. (**AFKing**)
    - This will result in a queue ban or a ban from accessing MCSR Ranked, ranging from 7 days to 30 days.

## C. Player Conduct
In MCSR Ranked, the standard is to respect your opponent and ensure they have a good experience. As long as you maintain good sportsmanship, you don't need to worry.

1. Do not be excessively toxic, use slurs, discriminate, or post NSFW content in the game chat.
    - This will result in a chat mute, ranging from 7 days to permanent.
    - Out-of-game incidents will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis. For example, hate speech in calls, in person, online etc.
2. Do not attempt to gain an unfair, informational advantage from your opponent through their stream. (**Stream sniping**)
    - This will result in a ban from accessing MCSR Ranked, ranging from 7 days to 30 days, depending on the severity of the offense.

## D. Playoffs
Violations of the guidelines may result in a ban from attaining phase points or attending playoffs for a number of seasons, for more severe cases.
Violations during playoffs-related matches will be treated much more harshly.

## E. Ban / Mute Appeals
For now, appeals should be made via tickets in the [MCSR Ranked Discord](https://mcsrranked.com/discord).
Successful appeals will reduce the duration of the punishment or lift the punishment entirely.

**Appealling a punishment does not mean it will be revoked. It means we will look again at your case, the context, and the way you conduct yourself.**
